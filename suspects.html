How to play the Game

  1. Firstly, read up on the events of the crime, learn and memorise the circumstances.
  2. Next, the police have given you their 9 suspects linked to the crime.
  3. By navigating through the suspects backstories, the clues and witness statments - you must pick your main suspect.
  4. But be warned, you only have 2 guesses and if you get both wrong - you lose.
  5. There is only 1 true main suspect. Good luck.
