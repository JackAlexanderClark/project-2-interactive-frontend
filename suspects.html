How to play the Game

  1. Firstly, read up on the events of the crime, learn and memorise the circumstances.
  2. Next, the police have given you their 9 suspects linked to the crime.
  3. By navigating through the suspects backstories, the clues and witness statments - you must pick your main suspect.
  4. But be warned, you only have 2 guesses and if you get both wrong - you lose.
  5. There is only 1 true main suspect. Good luck.

The Crime

  • At approximately 1:30am your house was petrol bombed.
  • You were knocked unconcious, you've been in a coma - your memories are missing.
  • A figure was seen loitering the area, at 12:00am.
  • A black leather wallet was left at the crime scene.
  • Police have interviewed multiple witnesses. You can read their statements.
  • Several items were discovered at the scene. You can inspect the clues.
